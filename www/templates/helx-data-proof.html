SATISFACTION

At the end of a test task, sonographers rated the ease of use of completing that task.

For all but one task*, the task-by-task ease-of-use ratings for Siemens scored the same or higher
than the competitors.

*TASK 4A was rated lower than the competitors due to difficulties using the dual screen.

EFFECTIVENESS

For all tasks except one*,

SIEMENS’ usability tests outperformed the competition, consistently scoring lower than or equal to competitors’ usability tests regarding the rate observed errors.

Across all tasks,

SIEMENS' usability tests were the only tests that showed no notable differences regarding number of observed deviations.

*TASK 4A was rated lower than the competitors due to difficulties using the dual screen.

PREFERENCE

When asked to provide their preference between systems at the end of the test sessions, 15 out of 20 sonographers preferred the SIEMENS system.
