Description

Premium performance. Extraordinary technology.

The ACUSON S3000™ ultrasound system is the most advanced member of the ACUSON S Family™ ultrasound systems, combining top-of-the-line performance with user-friendly technology.

Explore the following hotspots to learn more about the unique features of the ACUSON S3000 system.

eSie Fusion Imaging Technology

A pioneering technology from Siemens, eSie Fusion™ technology allows for the precise alignment of 3D topographies from MRI or CT with real-time ultrasound imagery. The combination of these two technologies represents new strides in providing more complete and comprehensive diagnostic imagery.

eSie Guide Needle Tracking

This innovative feature provides real-time 3D ultrasound navigational guidance using segmentation tools, needle-trajectory markers, and 3D markers for regions of interest. Along with eSie Fusion imaging, it can reduce the risk associated with biopsies or ablation procedures.

Workflow Innovations

The ACUSON S3000 system offers no-look operation and user-automated protocols. It also features multi-modality review, which provides side-by-side review of mammography, CT, or MRI with live ultrasound for improved exam efficiency. The ACUSON S3000 further streamlines your workflow with Nuance PowerScribe® 360 Reporting, which supports seamless data transfer into high-quality reports.

HD Transducers

The ACUSON S3000 system offers a variety of high-density transducers, including advanced transducers for General Imaging and Interventional Radiology. These tools feature superior penetration, increased resolution, and an expanded view for a wide variety of parts. They are also designed for usability and comfort.
