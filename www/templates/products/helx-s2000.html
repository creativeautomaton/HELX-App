Description

Premium performance. Expanded insights.

The ACUSON S2000™ ultrasound system is designed with versatility in mind, offering exceptional imaging and performance across a variety of imaging applications.

Explore the following hotspots to learn more about the unique features of the ACUSON S2000 system.

ABVS

The ACUSON S2000™ Automated Breast Volume Scanner (ABVS) is designed to deliver superior care for women, especially those with heterogeneously or radiographically dense breast tissue. The system provides consistent ultrasound results and offers tissue strain analytics and insights into dense breast tissue.

Virtual Touch: Virtual Touch IQ

The ACUSON S2000 system comes equipped with Virtual Touch™ IQ, a complementary application of Acoustic Radiation Force Impulse (ARFI) technology and quantitative measurement. Designed to provide highly sensitive and specific high-resolution imaging and quantitative analysis of tissue stiffness, this technology provides a new dimension of diagnostic imaging.

Workflow Innovations

The ACUSON S2000 system features Nuance PowerScribe® 360 Reporting, which supports seamless data transfer into high-quality reports, eliminating all manual transcription by allowing measurement-value dictation. In addition, syngo® technology reduces exam time with automated tracing, landmarks, and measurements.

Transducers

The ACUSON S2000 system includes high-density transducers that deliver more ultrasound data, color sensitivity, and the highest detail resolution. Insights are displayed on a 1080p high-definition monitor powered by SieStream™ HD Architecture, which promotes optimal image clarity.
