Description

Premium performance. Exceptional value.

Designed to foster an improved ultrasound experience and promote confidence in meeting your daily imaging needs, the ACUSON S1000™ ultrasound system offers high-quality imaging with a user-friendly design.

Explore the following hotspots to learn more about the unique features of the ACUSON S1000 system.

Virtual Touch: eSie Touch Elasticity Imaging

Ideal for breast imaging due to its inherent sensitivity for assessing lesions in breast tissue, eSie Touch™ elasticity imaging displays and calculates relative tissue stiffness in real time.

Cadence CHI

With improved Cadence™ contrast harmonic imaging (CHI)1 and contrast pulse sequencing (CPS)1, the ACUSON S1000 system delivers enhanced separation between tissue and contrast agents. These advanced features promote image clarity and confident diagnoses.

1. The U.S. Food and Drug Administration has cleared ultrasound contrast agents only for use in LVO. Check the current regulation for the country in which you are using this system for contrast agent clearance.

Workflow Innovations

By automatically adjusting gain and reducing noise in real time, eSieImage™ multiparametric optimization reduces exam time and increases operator comfort. The ACUSON S1000 system also promotes an efficient workflow through Nuance PowerScribe® 360 Reporting, which supports seamless data transfer into high-quality reports, eliminating all manual transcription by allowing measurement-value dictation.

Transducers

The ACUSON S1000 system offers a wide range of transferable transducers, including the 18L6 HD transducer. Available on the ACUSON S1000 system, S2000 system, and S3000 system, the 18L6 HD delivers exquisite detail and contrast resolution for the breast and small parts. Each transducer is designed with the user in mind and features improved ergonomics for more comfortable operation.
